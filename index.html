Can you give me a short overview of your career so far and how you've ended up where you are now?

  • Mobile developer at Techster, a small company with 4 developers at hiring that grew to 20 during tenure
  • Two-year tenure at Techster
  • Joined Google in California, focusing on Google search
  • Predominantly worked as a software engineer throughout career
⠿

How did you find the transition from working at a small place to a big company?

  • Enjoyed Techster despite limitations; team became close friends but similar backgrounds hindered growth
  • Google transition provided access to many people with different experiences and daily learning opportunities
  • Large company downsides include rigid quarterly OKRs with minimal flexibility for direction changes
  • Described feeling like a small cog in a large machine
⠿

What is a specific project that stands out as particularly challenging for you as a software developer?

  • Mobile app for healthcare client tracking patient data with real-time updates for providers
  • HIPAA compliance required extensive security measures; encryption and authentication for authorized access
  • Robust, scalable database schema for massive data; caching and indexing for fast response under load
  • Agile methodology; close team collaboration on encryption/authentication research
⠿

Which technologies did you use for that project?

  • React Native for the mobile app
  • Firebase for the backend
  • AWS for security and cloud storage
⠿

What are your salary expectations?

  • Salary expectations between $80,000 and $100,000
⠿

Are you interested in equity?

  • Yes, interested in equity as a big reason to join a startup
